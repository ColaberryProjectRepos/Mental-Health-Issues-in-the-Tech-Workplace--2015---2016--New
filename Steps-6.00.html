Suggestions / Recommendations: 6
  • Incorporate Mental Health Benefits into Workplace Healthcare Coverage: As seen in Texas, in 2016, employers offering mental health benefits had increased to 70% whereas in 2015 the percentage was at a low of 35%. I recommend employers partnering with insurance providers, that have an extensive network of mental health professionals, to ensure mental health coverage is comprehensive and includes a wide range of services, including therapy, counseling, psychiatric care, and substance abuse treatment .
  • Implement targeted interventions:  28% of age group 26-30 felt there would be negative consequences if mental health issues were known. On Average across the U.S., employees willing to discuss mental health issues with employers/coworkers was at a low of 25%. I recommend identifying specific mental health disorders to help figure out how to decrease mental health issues in the workplace. This could involve suggestion boxes in order to address concerns while allowing one to remain anonymous, one hour group sessions during quarterly lunches, and allowing employees to work remotely to support work-life balance can help reduce stress.   
  • Telehealth Options: In the Tech world, since there is flexibility working remotely, I recommend exploring the implementation of programs and benefits tailored to specific age groups, particularly targeting employees in the 20-40 age range. Offering telehealth options for mental health consultations to improve accessibility and convenience for employees, particularly those in remote locations or with scheduling constraints would be convenient.
  • Share best practices and benchmarks: The data shows a decrease in employees unwilling to discuss mental health issues in the Tech Workplace, across the United States from 2015-2016. I suggest establishing relationships between employers and employees could open the door to a stress free environment. This can be done by monitoring participation rates and gathering feedback to determine the effectiveness of peer support initiatives in creating a supportive community.
