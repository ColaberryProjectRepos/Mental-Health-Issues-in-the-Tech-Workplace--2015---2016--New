Executive Summary

The upheaval caused by layoffs and career transitions can have a profound impact on the mental well-being of employees. Facing uncertainty, financial strain, and the pressure of re-entering a competitive job market can lead to increased stress, anxiety, and even feelings of depression. The loss of a familiar work environment, routine, and sense of purpose can further exacerbate these challenges, leading to a sense of helplessness and low self-esteem. In this project we will analyze respondents that suffered from a Mental Health Disorder between 2015-2016 and see what kind of impact this had on employees within the Tech Workplace.
