Transforming the Data in the Power Query Editor 9
In this step you will clean and transform the data:

1. Select Transform Data from Home tab.
2. In the Transform tab, click on "Use First Row as Headers".
3. Change the datatypes to the correct type.
4. Pivot columns X,Y,&Z (refer to Excel Spreadsheet) and make any other manipulations as necessary.
5. Then try to understand the data, because to start any analysis of data, it is crucial to understand the data from a business perspective.
6. Close & Apply to save changes.

**Please note this is a massive dataset. Results may change depending on manipulation**
