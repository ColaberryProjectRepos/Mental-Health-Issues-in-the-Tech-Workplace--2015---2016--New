Creating Age Group Line & Stacked Column Chart 20

  • Click on an empty space on the canvas, and from the visualizations pane select Line and Stacked Column Chart
    •  Fill up figures provided below for the necessary fields.
    • Build Visual Tab:
    •  X-axis = Age Group
    •  Column y-axis = Count of Speaking Openly
    •   Line y-axis = Cases YoY%, Awareness%, Benefits%, Discussions%, TakeSerious%.
    • Column Legend = What is your gender
    •   Tooltips = Cases YoY%, Awareness%, Benefits%, Discussions%, TakeSerious%
    • Tooltip = Keep all filters on.
